My Work

Temple hub

This project is developed in order to digitalize the Abhishieks which are carried out at sacred Temples of lord shiva.

The system allows the general people living far away from these places to register for havan there without reaching physically.

After the verification of user using basic credentials, he/she eligible for havan at any of 5 jyotirlinga's of Shiva.

The user just need to register themselves to the system by inputting the basic credentials to perform havan/Pooja.

There is also a feedback form to get the feedback of users about their experince of Service.

This site is developed and successfully deployed by me with the contribution of Rakshanda Giri and Shrutika Mahajan.
